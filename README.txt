Fake Store Shopping Cart Application
This project is a ReactJS-based Shopping Cart Application that fetches products from the Fake Store API, 
displays them on the product page, and allows users to add and remove products from the cart. 

Fetch products from Fake Store API.
Display products in a responsive grid layout.
View product image, title, price, and description.
Add products to the cart.
Remove products from the cart.
Increase or decrease the quantity of products in the cart.
Calculate total price dynamically according to quantity.
Apply 10% discount on the total price.
React Router for navigation between Product Page and Cart Page.
